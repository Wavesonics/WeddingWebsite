Capturing the day

We want to capture as much of the weekend as possible,
so we've put together a few ways for you to share your photos with us!

Party Mode!

Automatically upload photos to our event as you take them throughout the day! This is by far the easiest way if you have an Android phone we think. If you RSVP to our G+ Event then on the day of the wedding your phone will pop-up an option to enter "Party Mode". If you opt-in, every picture you take while at the event will be automatically uploaded so we can all enjoy them!
