Capturing the day

We want to capture as much of the weekend as possible,
so we're putting together a few ways for you guys to get photos to us that you may have taken.

Party Mode!

Automatically upload photos to our Event as you take them throughout the day! This is by far the easiest way we think. If you RSVP to our G+ Event, then on the day of the wedding, your phone will pop-up an option to enter "Party Mode". If you opt-in, every picture you take while at the event will be uploaded along wither everyone else's so we can all enjoy them!
