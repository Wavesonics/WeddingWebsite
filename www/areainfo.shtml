Gold Bar, WA

what to do & where to sleep

Are there hotels in this tiny little town? Heck yeah!


Wallace Falls Cabins

Right next door to our venue! Rustic cabins that sleep up to five adults.  They can be reserved up to 9 months in advance and always fill up early, especially on holiday weekends! Additionally, there are two walk up tent sites adjacent to the cabins.

Stevens Pass Motel

A rustic country cabin style motel. This budget friendly lodging is very close by, just two miles from the lodge. Located within walking distance to La Hacienda Mexican restaurant and a local grocer.

The Dutch Cup Motel

In nearby Sultan, WA the pet-friendly Dutch up motel offers online reservations, WiFi and many other amenities. It is approximately a 10 minute drive from the lodge. Additionally, they offer military and AAA member discounts.

Guesthouse Inn & Suites

Guesthouse Inn & Suites, is the furthest option from the lodge at about a 25 minute drive, but provides a more upscale experience. Amenities include an exercise room, indoor pool and complimentary continental breakfast.

On site camping

Wallace Falls Lodge welcomes our guests to stay on site and will allow up to five tents.  The tents will be set up on the grounds out of the way of wedding photography.  Please contact us directly if you would like to camp so we can keep track of the number of tents. BYOT!

Nature!

Snohomish County is home to endless outdoor adventures.  Whether it's an adrenaline filled raft trip down the Skykomish River, an afternoon of fishing for Steelhead Trout and Salmon,  a short hike up to Wallace Falls, or relaxing and taking in the scenery-- there's something for everyone!
