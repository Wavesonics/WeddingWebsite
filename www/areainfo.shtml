Gold Bar, WA

what to do & where to sleep

Are there hotels in this tiny little town? Heck yeah!


Wallace Falls Cabins

Right next door to our venue! Rustic cabins that sleep up to five adults.  They can be reserved up to 9 months in advance and always fill up early, especially on holiday weekends! Additionally, there are two walk up tent sites adjacent to the cabins.

Stevens Pass Motel

This is the next closest option, just two miles from the lodge. 

The Dutch Cup Motel

Another option.  It is approximately a 10 minute drive from the lodge.

Guesthouse Inn & Suites

Further away (~25 min drive) but provides a more upscale experience.

On site camping

also an option for up to five tents.  The tents will be set up on the grounds out of the way of wedding photography.  Please contact us directly if you would like to camp so we can keep track of the number of tents.

Nature!

Snohomish County is home to endless outdoor adventures.  Whether it's an adrenaline filled raft trip down the Skykomish River, an afternoon of fishing for Steelhead Trout and Salmon,  a short hike up to Wallace Falls, or relaxing and taking in the scenery-- there's something for everyone!

The Reptile Zoo

Nearby Monroe, WA offers an affordable, family friendly animal encounter! The reptile zoo is home to lots of snakes, turtles, bugs and even an albino alligator! RAWR!

Rafting!

Alpine Adventures offers heart thumping white water rafting as well as relaxing calm water wildlife watching floats, perfect for photography!  They also offer white water kayaking and stand up paddleboard instruction.
