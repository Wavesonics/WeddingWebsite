The Lodge

Wallace Falls Lodge is a family friendly venue featuring ten bedrooms, a home style kitchen, and plenty of space to sit and visit. The grounds include a large lawn, horseshoe pit, fire pit and barbecue grills. The Lodge accommodates up to 28 overnight  guests.

We invite our immediate family and bridal party to spend the weekend with us and get to know one another in a relaxed atmosphere.
