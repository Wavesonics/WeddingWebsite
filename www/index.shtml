How did you people meet?

Good question. We met in 2006 in beautiful Plymouth, NH. Since then we've lived in Dover, NH, Boston, MA and now, Bellevue, WA.

So now  you're getting married?

Yes! We got engaged at 12,300' on Mt. Rainier as the sun was coming up.  It was a beautiful and perfect proposal.

Who is this adam guy, anyway?

Adam Brown, son of Merrill and Deborah Brown, grew up in Freehold, New Jersey and attended Plymouth State University, where he met Stacy.  He is a software engineer, currently working at Bungie in Bellevue, WA.  He loves the outdoors and is an avid hiker, skier and nature lover.  He is super handsome and has a sweet, sweet ass.

And What about Stacy?

Stacy Rahaim, daughter of Gary and Donna Rahaim, grew up in Gardner, Massachusetts.  She attended Plymouth State University, where she met Adam.  She worked as a nanny for many years and has recently returned to school to complete a BS in Nursing.  She will graduate two weeks before the wedding.  She might be crazy.
